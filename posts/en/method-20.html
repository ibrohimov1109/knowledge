METHODS

20 basic methods

Below are the basic methods used for network marketing and team development in a consistent and understandable way. These methods are based on practical experience and give stable results when applied correctly.

1. Buyers method

The minimum purchase is 150,000 UZS, and the buyer gets a 20% discount.

2. Salesmanship method

This is a method of teaching consultants to work through orders.

3. Online work method

Distribution of advertisements via Telegram, Instagram, and YouTube channels. There are two directions: business sales and product sales.

4. Method of talking to contacts

Teaches proper communication with new and existing contacts.

5. Master class method

A training method conducted live or via video.

6. Recruit and "home circle" method

Attracting new partners and team work at home.

7. Method of creating a leader from the first conversation

Aims at forming a leader at the initial stage of conversation.

8. Travel method

Incentives through free trips provided by the company.

9. Business lady method

A method designed for women, aimed at self-development and leadership.

10. Future and dream-goal method

Waking up sleeping dreams, being able to show the future hidden by obstacles.

11. Method based on need

Identifying the partner's needs during conversation and directing accordingly.

12. Method of clearing the mind from questions

Answering important business questions even if the partner doesn't ask.

13. Amazement method

Talking about high achievers in the company or personal successes.

14. Choice without choice method

Boldly registering close, warm contacts and teaching the work. Showing that this job is very easy and suitable for everyone.

15. Method of befriending opinions

Approving the interlocutor's opinion, saying you used to think so too, and gently directing them to a positive opinion.

16. Resourcefulness method

The knowledge base should be so broad that you can answer every question fluently and boldly without thinking. Remember, those who ask many questions are future leaders.

17. Method of influencing in one attempt

Listening carefully to the interlocutor and influencing based on their interests, dreams, and needs.

18. Method of finding a new "lemon"

A method of creating a leader from a leader. Very effective and leads to great growth when applied correctly.

19. Method of convincing without inviting

Getting people to invite themselves by having interesting live or phone conversations. Often strong leaders emerge from those who came by their own choice.

20. Method of launching new consultants

Immediately establishing a relationship with a new registered partner and finding a product suitable for their needs. If there is fear, the "Osh pishirish" method gives good results.
