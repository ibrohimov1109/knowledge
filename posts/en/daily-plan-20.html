EFFECTIVE AND BLESSED DAILY PLAN

20 Practical Rules

The daily plan below is based on proven practical rules that deliver consistent results in network marketing. Following this plan regularly leads to business growth, leadership development, and abundance.

1. Morning prayers

Perform prayers in the morning — every day.

2. A new idea every day

Work on at least one new thought or idea per day — every day.

3. Warm relationships with the team

Kind words, promotion, and motivation within the team — every day.

4. Communicating with contacts

Communicate with new and existing contacts — every day.

5. Recruiting

Invite new people — twice a week.

6. Home meetings

Organize home-based meetings — once a week.

7. Relationships with leaders

Communication and conversations with leaders — five times a day.

8. One-on-one talks with consultants

Individual conversations with consultants in the team — twice a week.

9. Master classes

Live or video master classes — three times a week.

10. Follow-ups

Re-contact people you spoke with yesterday or those who were partially interested, and re-engage them — every day.

11. Working with special groups

Create separate groups for leaders who choose business-lady or travel directions. • Share new ideas daily, • Provide new information, • Stay in constant communication. Best time: evening, every day.

12. Keeping a daily planner

Maintain a daily planner consistently. At least 30 tasks should be completed per day — every day.

13. Giving charity

Based on your ability, give charity even if it is small. Making someone happy with kind words also counts as charity — every day.

14. Studying the habits of billionaires

Work on yourself and learn to think big — every day.

15. Developing new leaders from leaders

Develop new leaders through existing leaders — every day.

16. Learning products

Study at least one product per day — every day.

17. Offline training sessions

Organize offline meetings and training sessions — once per catalog.

18. One-on-one meetings

Individual conversations and analysis with leaders. At least 5 people per week — every day.

19. Reminding goals

Remind leaders of their goals. If possible — every day.

20. Business meetings and recognition

Hold business meetings with leaders in offices or cafes and conduct recognition events — once per catalog.

Final thought

If this daily plan is followed consistently: • the business operates systematically, • the team becomes stronger, • results and abundance appear.
